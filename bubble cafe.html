Our Story

Born in Surat,
Made for Everyone

Teaspresso was born from a simple belief — that every visit to a café should feel like a small celebration. From our first bubble tea to our signature waffle creations, we craft each order with intention, quality, and a whole lot of heart.

🌿

100% Vegetarian — Always

Every ingredient is thoughtfully sourced and completely vegetarian. No compromise, no exceptions.

👨‍🍳

Made Fresh to Order

We never pre-make or reheat. Your drink and food is prepared fresh the moment you order it.

✨

The Experience Matters

From the neon-lit ambiance to every plate's presentation — we obsess over the details.
